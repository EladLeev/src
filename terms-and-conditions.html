Personal data

  1. The Organizer is the administrator of the Personal Data provided by Participants.
  2. The Organizer stores the following data (as requested in the Registration Form):

    2. Last Name
    3. Email Address
    4. Job Title / Main interest
    5. Phone Number
    6. Company / University / Community
    7. Country
    8. Any additional information provided by the Participant during registration
  3. The Organizer processes Personal Data in connection with participation of the Participant in the Conference, on the basis of voluntary consent of the Participant, which is necessary to participate in the Conference, in accordance with applicable law.
  4. The Participant has the right to inspect and correct their Personal Data. Providing correct personal data is necessary to take part in the conference.
